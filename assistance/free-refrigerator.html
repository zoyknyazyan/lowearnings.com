LADWP’s Refrigerator Exchange Program

LADWP’s Refrigerator Exchange Program allows you to exchange your old refrigerator for a new one.

The following conditions must be met to be eligible for the refrigerator exchange: –

  • The applicant must have a refrigerator that falls within the service region of the LADWP.
  • At the time of his application, the applicant had owned the refrigerator for ten years.
  • The refrigerator must cover 14 cubic feet of space.
  • The refrigerator must be possessed by the organization, a renter, or the property owner.
  • It must be utilized as a primary unit and not kept in storage.
  • The refrigerator was in functioning order at the time of the transaction.
  • The refrigerator should be hooked into a properly grounded outlet.
